About Us



Patient management can be efficiently done both on online and offline consultation using this system. 
According to the preference of the patient, the consultation can be online or offline.During this 
pandemic, there is also a need for online consultation where the patient can log in to their account 
and book an appointment. Or they can provide their details at Out Patient Department where the token 
is generated.Next, they will be assigned to the concerned doctor.The Doctor checks up on their illness
and reads through the previous medical records. Then the doctor either prescribes medicine or assigns 
treatment for further diagnosis. According to the illness, the patient is admitted in the beds along 
with care taker.This might also require a lab test for detailed analysis. The patient report and the 
bill will be generated once when the patient is being discharged from the hospital.Any time patients 
can log in to their account and find their test results, bills, medication needed to be followed.
